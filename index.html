Mission Statement

It is time to get unstuck and move toward a more prosperous society. There are excellent online resources offering free courses, bootcamps, and certifications. My motivation is to help people from Honduras discover and access these opportunities so they can learn new skills and knowledge, unlock their full potential, and pursue better opportunities to improve their lives and communities. Since most of these free programs are in English, the goal of this organization is to help participants gain enough English proficiency to complete these educational programs independently or with minimal assistance in a short period of time.
